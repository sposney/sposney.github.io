CALIBRATION

Calibration of the ARC device is done in four phases - bending forward, backward, left, and right. This is
done to set thresholds for your movements and set a baseline
for fatigue index calculation.

For each phase, stand with your feet shoulder width apart and keep your back straight. Bend slowly in the appropriate direction until reaching your threshold position, and then return to the starting position. Confirm the measured threshold reading before proceeding to the next phase.
